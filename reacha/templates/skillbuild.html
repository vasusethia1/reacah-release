ABOUT THE PROJECT

REACHA has partnered with IBM Skillsbuild - The launch pad to create the next chapter in careers through learning experience, and community, using technology as the enabler. For more details Click Here

Over the next 12 months upto August 2021, as part of SkillsBuild, REACHA will reach out across the country to engage and train 3000 young women and men in India for meaningful employment/placement. This would be part of PM Sri Narender Modi's efforts to impart 21st century IT skills to a young nation

If you are keen to partner us in this nation-building effort, please drop an email to nikhil@reacha.org

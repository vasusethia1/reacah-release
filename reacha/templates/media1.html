SDGs THROUGH CSR

Decoding the Voluntary National Report (VNR) 2020 on SDGs - 'Decade of Action: Taking SDGs from Global to Local’

A wonderful discussion took place on Saturday 29 August 2020 involving NITI Aayog, Global Compact Network - India, Sattva Consulting, CSR Spark and HCL Foundation.

REACHA as CSO (Civil Society Organisation) partner facilitated a dialogue on the Voluntary National Report (VNR) 2020 that was presented by NITI Aayog at the UN this July 2020. Key deliberations focused on the role CSR can play to strengthen efforts to achieve stated impact parameters across Sustainable Development Goals SDGs - through collaboration, convergence and co-creation between all stakeholders - Government, Corporates, Civil Society and Communities. Thank you to Mr. Muniraju SB Deputy Advisor, NITI Aayog, Dr. Bhaskar Chatterjee (IAS Retd.), Former DG & CEO, IICA Ms. Nidhi Pundhir, Director HCL Foundation, Mr. Srikrishna Sridhar Murthy, CEO, Sattva Consulting Limited, Mr. Kamal Singh, Executive Director, UN GCNI, Mr. Aseem Kumar, Head Asia Pacific Resource Center, UN GCNI and Mr. RK Krishnaswamy, Founder CSR Spark
